Ilesa, Osun State, Nigeria

Ilesa is a major city in Osun State, Nigeria, and the headquarters of Ijeshaland: Ilesa is in a rainforest zone and is located at the intersection of roads to Ile Ife, Akure, and Osogbo. Ilesa is known for its agricultural potential and is a major collecting point for cocoa, palm oil, yams, cassava, corn, pumpkins, cotton, and kola nuts. It also has local industries that manufacture nails and carpets. Ilesa is the capital of a historic state-kingdom also known as Ijesha. The Ilesa royal families claim descent from Oba Oduduwa.
