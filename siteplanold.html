Dog World - Website Plan

A plan for an online pet food and accessories store.

Site Name

Dog World

This name is straightforward, memorable, and clearly communicates the focus of the website: products and information related to dogs.

[Placeholder for Dog World Logo]

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Dog World website is to serve as an online store for dog owners to conveniently purchase high-quality dog food, treats, toys, and other essential accessories. The site will also aim to be a resource for dog owners, providing information and potentially a blog with articles on dog care, nutrition, and training tips.

  • Offer a wide selection of dog food brands and types.
  • Provide a variety of dog accessories, including collars, leashes, beds, and grooming supplies.
  • Enable easy online ordering and secure payment processing.
  • Offer delivery services to [Specify Target Area, e.g., Ilesa and surrounding areas].
  • Potentially include a blog or resource section for dog owners.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "My dog has a sensitive stomach. What grain-free food options do you have and what are the customer reviews like?"
  • Scenario 2: "I just got a new puppy. What are some essential accessories I should buy, and do you have any training tips for new puppy owners?"

Color Schema

The color schema will aim for a friendly, trustworthy, and pet-focused feel.

  • Primary Color: #A8DADC (A light, calming teal - used for headings, primary buttons, and potentially as an accent color)
  • Secondary Color: #457B9D (A deeper, trustworthy blue - used for background sections, important text, and navigation links)
  • Accent Color: #F1FAEE (An off-white - used for primary text and content backgrounds to provide contrast)
  • Neutral Color: #E6E6E6 (A light gray - used for borders, subtle backgrounds, and secondary text)

Typography

The typography will aim for readability and a slightly friendly, approachable tone.

  • Primary Heading Font: Raleway (Sans-serif - used for main headings (h1, h2) for a modern and clean look)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (Sans-serif - used for paragraphs and general text for excellent readability)
  • Accent Font: Pacifico (Cursive - used sparingly for the site name/logo or decorative elements to add a touch of personality)
