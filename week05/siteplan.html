Site Purpose

DOG WORLD is a one-stop retail outlet providing wide range solutions to dog owners. We offer varieties of Local and Foreign Dog Food, Foreign and Local Dewormers, Multivitamins and Tonics, Ticks and Fleas Spray, etc. We also offer Dog Accessories, Cages / Kennels, Toys and Treats. We made home delivery services our selling points, offering routine vaccination, grooming and training. What endears us to our customers are the good quality of our products and services together with the pocket friendly prices that we offer. We educate and assist dog owners in our quest to improve the lives of their cherished dogs; creating healthier dogs and happier dog owners. Our home delivery services cover both private and commercial dog owners, including the private security outfits within the town.
